What Did I Learn from This Chapter?

The chapter Statistical Inference I introduced me to the methods used to draw meaningful conclusions about a population based on information obtained from a sample.

I gained knowledge of key concepts such as estimation, confidence intervals, and hypothesis testing. The chapter taught me how to formulate null and alternative hypotheses, perform statistical tests, and interpret results to make informed decisions.
